About Gloria Zhang

My Name is Xinhui Zhang, but you can call me Gloria. I enjoy drawing and painting since the first day I could pick up a pencil. In 2015, I graduated from the Art Institute of Seattle. I love making things with my hands, but I realized that fashion is way more than art, and it does not best present who I am. Therefore, after a year of working in retail, I decided to pursue computer related fields. I'm currently a full time student at Seattle Central College working on Computer Programming associate degree.
